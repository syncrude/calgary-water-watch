Why don't the leaks stop?

During the 2024 and 2026 water crises, Citizens and Regional partners (Airdrie, Chestermere) reduced usage by ~25% to save the system. However, Industry stayed mostly flat due to operational exemptions, and Leaks remained constant at ~115 Million Litres/Day. Conservation by citizens cannot fix holes in the City's pipes.
