Adjusted Daily Volume Logic

The volumes above are calculated by applying distribution ratios to the Total Day Demand for the current record. According to City data, 22% of total demand is lost to infrastructure leaks. The remaining usage is shared between Residential (60%), ICI (31%), and Regional partners (8%).

Data Methodology:
Baseline Leaks: 22% of Total Day Demand.
Sector Distribution (of usage): 60% Residential, 31% Industrial/Commercial, 8% Regional.
Source: City of Calgary "CEAS – Water Demand Daily" data stream.
